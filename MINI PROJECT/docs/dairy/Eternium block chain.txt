Eternium block chain
Eternium NFT is a decentralized financial payment network that rebuilds the traditional payment stack on the blockchain. It utilizes a basket of fiat-pegged stablecoins, algorithmically stabilized by its reserve currency Eternium, to facilitate programmable payments and open financial infrastructure development.

Key Features

Decentralized financial payment network
Utilizes a basket of fiat-pegged stablecoins
Algorithmically stabilized by reserve currency Eternium
Facilitates programmable payments and open financial infrastructure development
Has transacted an estimated $299 billion for over 2 million users as of December 2020
